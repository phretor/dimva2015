Call for Posters

DIMVA 2015 is soliciting submissions of posters describing work in progress or innovative ideas in an early stage. We especially encourage thought-provoking approaches from students to reexamine older assumptions made in the community. We also welcome submissions from papers accepted into the proceedings to also be presented and discussed as a poster to further encourage the interactions between the speakers and the attendees at the conference.

Poster submissions need to be formatted as an extended abstract (one page) with a PDF of the proposed poster as the second page. Poster titles should be prefixed with “Poster abstract”.

Accepted posters can then be presented (printed on a A0 paper) at the conference during the welcome reception on July 8th.

Important Dates

  • Poster submission deadline: June 2, 2015
  • Poster notification: June 7, 2015
  • Poster session: July 8th afternoon, 2015
  • Main conference: July 9-10, 2015
